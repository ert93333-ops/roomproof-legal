Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

RoomProof is a renter room condition checklist and report app. This policy explains what data the current app uses and how it is handled.

Photos and room information

  • Camera or photo access is requested only after you choose a room documentation action.
  • Selected or captured photos are used to help create a room condition checklist and report inside the app.
  • The current release keeps room photos and notes on the device unless you choose to export or share a report.
  • The current release does not upload room photos to a server.
  • Room photos are not sold and are not shared with advertisers.

Purchases and ads

  • RoomProof may use App Store purchases through RevenueCat to unlock Pro features.
  • RoomProof may show rewarded ads only after the user chooses an ad-supported action.
  • Purchase status is used only to unlock app features and restore access.

Accounts, contacts, and location

  • No account is required for the current release.
  • Contacts are not uploaded or accessed.
  • Precise location is not required for the current release.

Retention and control

  • Local room notes, checklists, and reports remain on the device until you delete them or remove the app.
  • You can delete local reports from inside the app where deletion controls are provided.
  • Exported or shared reports are controlled by the destination app or service you choose.
